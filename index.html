How to use:

Click "start practicing using the sequences above" if you wish to use the default practice sequences. You can also enter your own sequences, described below.

As demonstrated in the default sequences above, you can add single characters or multiple characters to the sequence array, surrounded by double-quotes. If you want to practice with non-character keys such as the arrow keys, tab, etc, you can add them as specially-spelled words. They must be spelled exactly as you see here, and they must be separated by the interpunct: "·".

Click the "save to localstorage" button to save your custom training sequence to your browser's storage. Click "load from localstorage" to restore from your browser's storage.

NOTE! If you attempt to add control key sequences like "Alt-F4" or "Control-w" or "Control-n" and other such system or app-level combos, you WILL perform these actions (closing the window, the tab, opening a new tab, etc). These combos will vary for your browser and OS, so please take care. Also, you can't train on SHIFT alone... only in combos ala "Shift-ArrowUp" and of course upper-case characters and special characters ala "$".

Also Note: There is no "end". This will just loop through the given sequence forever.
